IREMIT, Inc

We are a company engaged in the business of servicing the remittance needs of overseas Filipinos for 2 decades. It is the only Philippine remittance company publicly listed in the Philippine Stock Exchange. I-Remit operates its own offices in 8 countries and is in partnership with more than 100 financial institutions in 30 countries across the globe.

Corporate Profile

  • Largest Filipino-owned non-bank remittance service provider
  • The only remittance company listed in The Philippine Stock Exchange, Inc.
  • The first to utilize the internet platform in order to provide a faster and affordable remittance service
  • Supervised and regulated by the Bangko Sentral ng Pilipinas (Manual of Regulations for Non- Bank Financial Institutions) as a Remittance and Transfer Company (BSP Circular 942, series of 2017)
  • Registered with the Board of Investments as a New Information Technology Service Firm in the field of Information Technology Services (Remittance Infrastructure System)
  • Growing network of remittance outlets consisting of subsidiaries, branches and tie-ups in 23 countries and territories in Asia Pacific, North America, Europe and Middle East
  • One of the widest distribution capabilities in the Philippines
