Creating an Account

How do I create an account?

Click "Join Now" and fill up your details in the registration form. A temporary password will be sent to your email. On your first login, reset your password. Watch the step-by-step video tutorial here.

What should I do if I forget my login credentials?

On the Sign-In page, click the 'Forgot Password' button. An email will be sent to you for the instructions on how to reset your password.

What are the payment methods available in my country?

The payment methods vary per country. Please contact Customer Support for more details.

How will my beneficiary receive money?

There are three different ways for your beneficiary to receive the money that you sent through iRemit.

iRemit Visa Card
• Simply send money by loading your beneficiary's iRemit Visa Card. In minutes, your money will be transferred and can be withdrawn by your beneficiary from all local ATMs in the Philippines..
Credit to bank account
• Through this delivery mode, your beneficiary will receive the money directly in his/her bank account. Remittance Pick-up
Remittance Pick-up
• Your beneficiary may pick-up anywhere from over 18,000 Pay-out Centers. No bank account required.

How long does it take for my beneficiary to receive the money?

The fastest way to receive the money in minutes is with the iRemit Visa Card. For other service modes,the average time for the money to be received by the beneficiary is within 1 to 2 banking days.

How do I track the status of my transfer?

You can track the status of your transfer by checking the "My History" Tab once you are logged into your account.

Approved:
• Your transaction is being processed for fulfillment to your beneficiary.
Available for Pick-up:
• If you sent via Remittance Pick-up, the remittance is now available for claiming by your beneficiary.
Credited:
• If you sent via Credit to Bank Account, the remittance has already been credited to the bank account you provided.
Delivered:
• Your remittance has already been claimed.

Remittance Fees & Exchange Rates

How much is the service charge?

iRemit fees depend on the country that you are sending from and the mode in which you want the money received by your beneficiary. Please use the cost estimator to calculate the estimated cost for your transaction.

What is the validity period for a locked-in rate?

Your rate will be locked in until 23:59:59 of the day that you confirmed your transaction following your timezone.

Security and Fraud Awareness

​What should I do if I receive an e-mail asking for my financial information?

​You should never respond to emails asking for financial information. Call iRemit and inform them of such email. iRemit should be contacted immediately should you suspect that you may be a victim of fraud.

​Where can I get more information on protecting myself from consumer fraud?

​​There are now websites that provide information and trends in consumer fraud. Please ensure you get the latest news and updates.

What should I do if someone calls claiming to be from iRemit and asks for my personal and financial information?

​​The security of your personal information is important to us. iRemit may give you a call to verify certain details regarding your transaction. If someone calls you claiming to be from iRemit and asks for your personal information, we recommend that you return the call using our toll free numbers provided on our website.
